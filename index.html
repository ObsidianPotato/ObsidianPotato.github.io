What is FruitBrawl?

In FruitBrawl, the shop is an obstacle, getting in the way of your dreams to kill your opponent. If you leave the fruit in the shop too long, they get overripe and start to attack you!

Don't let the cute art fool you, FruitBrawl is a highly competitive card game, made to have complex yet creative decision making.
